# Cabot Oil & Gas (cabot-oil-and-gas)
Cabot Oil & Gas was an independent oil and gas company engaged in the development, exploitation, exploration, production, and marketing of natural gas and oil properties in the United States. In 2021 it merged with Cimarex Energy to form Coterra Energy, which operates across the Permian Basin, Marcellus Shale, and Anadarko Basin.
